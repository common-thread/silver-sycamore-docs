## Points of Service

**Master Service Protocol - 23 Steps**

### Core Principle

**BE KIND AND SMILE... ALSO SPEAK LOUD WITH THE MASKS ON !!!!**

### Service Sequence

1. **Greet Guest**
   - Warm welcome as they enter

2. **Seat with Menu and Silverware**
   - Escort to table, provide menus and silverware

3. **Suggest starting with a Fun Drink and tell of special and soup of the day**
   - Promote beverage program and daily specials

4. **Get drink order (place order on a ticket immediately)**
   - Write down immediately to ensure accuracy

5. **Place alcohol order with bar**
   - Communicate bar orders promptly

6. **Take out drinks**
   - Serve beverages to table

7. **Take meal order**
   - Listen carefully and write clearly

8. **REPEAT ORDER! Ask if that is correct**
   - **Critical step** - confirm entire order with guest

9. **Make sure prints in kitchen**
   - Verify ticket printed at kitchen station

10. **Take out condiments needed for order**
    - Anticipate and provide necessary accompaniments

11. **Refill drinks before order goes out**
    - Keep beverages topped off

12. **Check order for correctness at the window (write time out on order ticket)**
    - Quality check before delivery, document timing

13. **Take out order, serve ladies first always and with protein in front on guest**
    - Proper service etiquette, protein placement

14. **Ask if anything else is needed**
    - Check for additional requests

15. **2 minute/2 bite taste test- after 2 minutes Ask guest if all to their liking**
    - Critical quality check window

16. **Refill drinks**
    - Maintain beverage service

17. **Tell guests of dessert for the day**
    - Promote dessert program

18. **Order dessert/clear plates/ take out desserts**
    - Sequential dessert service

19. **Print ticket/tell guests you will take care of it at the table when ready**
    - Prepare check, set clear expectations

20. **Clear plates if finished**
    - Remove finished dishware

21. **Check guests out at table**
    - Table-side payment processing

22. **Tell guests "Thank you and come see us again soon"**
    - Warm departure message

23. **Clean table and floor, sanitize and reset**
    - Prepare for next guests

## Cash Drawer Policy

**End of shift cash handling procedures**

### Critical Requirements:

1. **Servers cannot leave until cash drawer has been counted and all cash and receipts are accounted for**

2. **Servers will be required to make up any shortage from their cash tips before leaving for the day**

3. **Each server must have a printed copy of each paid receipt for guest tickets that will be matched to the corresponding order from the kitchen printer**
   - Ensures reconciliation between guest payments and kitchen orders
   - Prevents discrepancies and missing payments

## Guest Check Payment Policy

**Revised: 08-26-20**

### Payment Processing Protocol

1. **Servers must find out if guest check is to be split BEFORE taking orders**
   - Critical timing for split check management

2. **Servers must use first initial of first and last name before table numbers on tickets**
   - Example: JS-2
   - Ensures proper attribution and tracking

3. **Servers will process/print split check orders as follows:**
   - Send Initials, Table Number, (Seat) # of guests, A, B, C, etc. depending on number of guests
   - Example for a table of 4, all splitting separately:
     - JS-4a, JS2-4b, JS2-4c, JS2-4d

4. **When it's time to print the guest check/bill, look over the entire order to make sure you have charged for everything correctly**
   - **You will be held responsible for all items not paid at the end of your shift**

5. **Present the bill to the paying guest(s) let them know you will take their payment at the table when they are ready**
   - Table-side payment expectations

6. **ALL CREDIT/DEBIT card payments must be completed by the server, on your tablet, not at the front counter**
   - You are NOT BY THE FRONT DOOR
   - Server is responsible for swiping their credit/debit card and ensuring the payment has processed
   - You must verify with a printed receipt card

7. **If a guest is paying CASH, take their payment to the register, cash out the guest ticket and count your change TWICE before returning change to the guests**
   - Bring the payment to the register
   - Cash out the guest ticket
   - Count your change TWICE
   - Return change to guests
