# Pine Street Café at Silver Sycamore - Training Program

Comprehensive training schedule for new waitstaff at Pine Street Café.

**Source:** Client operational documents (2026-01-12)

---

## Onboarding Day

**Focus:** Administrative setup and orientation

1. Meeting with HR for paperwork
2. Introduction to the team
3. Review the handbook / Training manual and dress code
4. Review Menu
5. Review history of Silver Sycamore
6. Set up Square account / download app on phone
7. Set up Timestation account

---

## Day 1: Fundamentals

**Focus:** Basic operations and observation

1. Learn how to clock in and out
2. Review Menu / test on items
3. Learn Square register program
4. Study Inventory / locations of items
5. Observe Waitstaff
6. Learn Cleaning Duties

---

## Day 2: Opening Procedures

**Focus:** Shadow opening shift, learn full opening routine

### Shadow Waitstaff performing opening duties including:

#### Opening Duties Checklist:

- Clock in / log on Square App
- Adjust AC (70 for cold and 65 for heat)
- Turn Music on
- Make drip coffee, tea
- Check to see if restrooms, tables, and floor are clean
- Count register drawer
- Open drawer
- Write specials on outside board
- Roll minimum of 55 silverware packs **[Note: "done night before"]**
- Salt and Pepper shakers clean and full
- Condiments / Sugar bowls clean and full
- Get soup ready
- Turn on open sign at 8am and unlock door
- Freshen up and Restock Restroom with toilet paper and paper towels
- Cut lemons
- Make Fresh Orange Juice
- Make Lemonade
- Polish Glassware
- Get dog bowl ready

### Additional Tasks (Day 2 continued):

2. Work on Square register all day
3. Shadow a waiter or waitress all day
4. Learn closing duties

---

## Day 3: Advanced Training

**Focus:** Menu mastery and full shift duties

1. Menu Test
2. Preform all Opening Shift duties
3. Waitstaff for the day while being shadowed
4. Perform all shift closing duties

---

## Day 4: Independent Operation

**Focus:** Working independently with oversight

1. Menu Test
2. Preform all Opening Shift duties
3. Waitstaff for the day while being shadowed
4. Perform all shift closing duties

---

## Philosophy & Expectations

### Team Culture

> "By day 5 you should be a well-trained Waiter or Waitress. The one thing we can't train is your kindness and caring for people. However that is expected, so bring that in with you every day."

> "If you feel by the 3rd day you aren't going to get it or just don't like being a waiter or waitress, please let us know. It won't hurt our feelings. If you aren't happy doing what you do, our guests will feel it too."

> "We just don't want happy guests. We want a happy team!"

> "We are excited to have you as part of the Silver Sycamore Team!"

### Additional Cleaning Duties

**"IF YOU HAVE TIME TO LEAN YOUR HAVE TIME TO CLEAN:"**

- Polish all silverware from events and return to hall
- Wipe down all chairs, high chairs, and menus
- Wipe down all cabinets and walls
- Deep clean Fridge inside and out
- Dust window sills, fireplace, ceiling fans, and décor
- Clean Windows and front door glass with Windex
- Deep Clean Restrooms
- Sweep Front Porch
- Organize Bar area and polish glasses
- Check and notate Refrigerator temp AM and PM

---

## Training Success Metrics

- Menu knowledge (tested Days 3 & 4)
- Ability to perform opening duties independently
- Comfortable with Square POS system
- Understanding of cleaning standards
- Service quality while being shadowed
- Team integration and attitude
