# Small Party and Bridal and Baby Showers

## Silver Sycamore Tearoom Packages

Mixed Tea Bar: 3pm or 4-7pm
Price: $19.95 per person
Guest count: 25 - 45 guests

**Menu Includes:**
- Party Mix, Scones and Coffee
- Or
- Croissant Plate (chicken salad or tuna salad on croissants, chips and Pickle)
- Or
- Spinach and Bacon Quiche with petit salad

## Additional Offerings

Three decorated sheet cake (flavor of choice)
The price of cake depends on size of group and plating fee if you supply your cake.
Silver Sycamore has the right to $1.50 per person cutting/plating fee if you supply your cake.
You may also add more pastries to be in the bakery case for before event
First guest count and estimate to be made to us 10 days before event.
Any add more guests will be your minimum charge per person. The number of guests you guarantee will be your minimum charge per person. This is non-negotiable. You may add more guests the day of the party up to five.

**Final Payment of Guaranteed guest count is due 7 days prior to event.**

Payment can be cash, check, money order or credit card (not applicable to deposit)

Minimum fee added with credit card payments. Ask about our guest count of 25

**Deposit is $200.00**

We also offer small floral arrangements for the tables in the colors of the shower or party for an additional cost.
Ask about our macarons, beer, and wine.

Tax and 20% Gratuity is added to final price.
