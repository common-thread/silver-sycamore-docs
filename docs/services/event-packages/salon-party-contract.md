## Scheduling Notes

Please note certain dates fill up fast (especially around holidays like Valentine's Day, Mother's Day, Christmas etc.). It is best to book early.

- Any request to reschedule should be made with a 2-week notice to try to move to an alternate date, if availability still permits.
- The venue will try to be as accommodating as possible, but high-demand periods (e.g., Friday/Saturday, holidays) may have limited availability.

## Decorations Policy

- Decorations may be dropped off in advance (24-48 hours prior to event); arrangements can be made.
- The venue provides basic table settings. Additional specialty decor rentals are available upon request.
- Any outside decor must be approved. Items like confetti or glitter are prohibited.
