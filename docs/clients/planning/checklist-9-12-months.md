# Planning Checklist: 9-12 Months Out

Less than a year away! Here are the big items to tackle during this planning phase.

## Bridal Gown and Formal Wear

- [ ] Shop for and order bridal gown (new gowns take 6-16 weeks)
- [ ] Begin bridesmaid dress shopping (factor in their timeline too)
- [ ] Schedule fittings once dresses arrive

## Photography and Videography

- [ ] Research photographer styles you like
- [ ] Book photographer for engagement, bridal, and wedding day shoots

## Accommodations

- [ ] Block B&B rooms for wedding night if desired

## Save-the-Dates

- [ ] Order save-the-dates
- [ ] Compile guest address list
