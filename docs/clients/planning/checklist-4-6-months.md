# Planning Checklist: 4-6 Months Out

Now that the DJ and catering are settled and save-the-dates have been sent, it's time to focus on the details that make your day special.

## Cake and Desserts

- [ ] Schedule complimentary tasting with **Cakes by Jula** (included in most packages)
- [ ] Contact Jula at 832-932-1353 for design consultation

## Invitations and Stationery

- [ ] Order wedding invitations (use same vendor as save-the-dates for consistency)
- [ ] Prepare guest address list for mailing

## Decorations

- [ ] Schedule "Something Borrowed" room appointment at sycamorebrides@gmail.com
- [ ] Consider **Davenport Designs** for additional rentals (chargers, arches, centerpieces)
- [ ] Contact Angela at 281-908-2112 or ddevents.rentals@gmail.com

## Attire

- [ ] Rent tuxedos and formal wear for wedding party
- [ ] Schedule fittings for alterations

## Accommodations

- [ ] Book B&B or hotel rooms for out-of-town guests
- [ ] Reserve Silver Sycamore cottages or hotel rooms while available

## Events and Gifts

- [ ] Coordinate bridal shower with family or bridesmaids
- [ ] Check Silver Sycamore shower packages ($50 off for referrals)
- [ ] Send thank-you notes for all shower and wedding gifts
- [ ] Book honeymoon flights and hotels
- [ ] Consider **Chatman Destinations** at 877-638-6877 ext. 700

## Accessories

- [ ] Purchase ring pillow, toasting flutes, cake service set
- [ ] Get garter, guest book, picture frames
- [ ] Check "Something Borrowed" room for available items
