# Planning Checklist: 1-3 Months Out

Congratulations! It's only a few months away. Complete these final preparations to ensure your wedding day goes smoothly.

## Beauty and Makeup

- [ ] Hire your makeup artist (schedules fill up, book early)
- [ ] Consider **Mayra Malik** with **Simple Beauty** at 832-276-8668 or info@simplebeautyartistry.com

## Guest List and Seating

- [ ] Submit final guest count to Silver Sycamore **21 days before** your event
- [ ] Schedule your two-week-out appointment with coordinators
- [ ] Finalize seating arrangements

## Documents and Legalities

- [ ] Obtain your marriage license (forms available at www.missnowmrs.com)
- [ ] Plan for name change if applicable (The Knot offers packages for this)

## Vendors and Details

- [ ] Confirm florist orders for centerpieces and bouquets/boutonnieres
- [ ] Finalize music playlist for ceremony and reception
- [ ] Prepare bridal party introduction order
- [ ] Submit music selection at your 2-weeks-out appointment

## Celebrations

- [ ] Coordinate with Maid of Honor and Best Man on bachelor/bachelorette parties
- [ ] Plan rehearsal dinner (Silver Sycamore offers packages for after your rehearsal)
