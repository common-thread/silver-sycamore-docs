# Intimate Wedding Planning Reference

This document contains venue policies, restrictions, and frequently asked questions extracted from the intimate wedding planning worksheet. The complete interactive planning document is shared with clients via Google Sheets.

## Venue Policies

### Ceremony Restrictions

- **No real flower petals** in the Chapel
- **No open flames or candles** in the Chapel
- **Maximum 100 white garden chairs** for outdoor ceremonies
- Rehearsals can only be guaranteed the night before if Rehearsal Dinner is booked
- Rehearsals are scheduled based on availability, no later than 4pm
- All decor in Chapel or ceremony site must be removed at end of evening (nothing left overnight)

### Setup and Vendors

- Silver Sycamore does not provide ladders for vendor use
- Vendors must bring their own ladders and padding to protect surfaces
- All vendors and personal items must be removed within 30 minutes of event end
- Setup and pickup must fall within the rental period

### Reception Guidelines

| Location | Table Options |
|----------|---------------|
| Town (outdoors) | Round tables or "Aubrey Style" setup |
| Saloon | Round or banquet tables |

- Silver Sycamore will decide the day before to move outdoor receptions inside due to inclement weather
- Maximum 20 tables with a dance floor

### Dance Floor Sizes

| Size | Capacity |
|------|----------|
| Small (12x15) | Up to 100 guests |
| Medium (15x18) | Up to 150 guests |
| Large (18x24) | Up to 200 guests |
| Extra Large (24x30) | Over 200 guests |

### Table Seating

- Round tables seat 8-10 guests
- 6' banquet tables seat 6 guests
- Reserved tables recommended for family and bridal party

## Event Timeline (Typical)

Events follow this general flow:
1. Silver Sycamore opens
2. Arrival at Bridal Cottage / Jail
3. Decorating begins
4. First look photos
5. Ceremony begins
6. Bar opens
7. Bridal party announced / Formal photos
8. Dinner service
9. Cake cutting / Toasts
10. First dance / Special dances
11. Bouquet and garter toss
12. Grand exit
13. Bar closes / Event ends
14. Silver Sycamore gates close

## Important Reminders

### Day-Of Coordination

- A decision maker (not bride/groom/bridal party) should be designated for day-of questions
- Silver Sycamore staff may begin cleanup of ceremony area after formal photos
- Staff will enter Bridal Cottage and Jail to start cleanup before event ends (personal belongings untouched)
- Silver Sycamore does NOT provide ceremony music - bring your own phone/tablet (coordinator will queue songs)

### Decoration Tips

- Assign 5+ helpers for decorating (or 2-3 for simple setups)
- Set up 1-2 sample tables completely so helpers know what to do
- Decorating typically takes 1 hour with venue linens, 1.5 hours with outside linens
- If using Silver Sycamore linens, they will be on tables upon arrival
- Extra linens (runners, overlays, chair ties) must be given to coordinator day before and removed same night

### Exit Requirements

- Assign someone to remove all decor items
- Assign someone to clear Bridal Cottage and Jail

### Children and Safety

- Children must be supervised at all times
- No children allowed near the pond
- Children must not climb on rocks
- A responsible adult contact required for overall child supervision

### Other Policies

- No smoking except in designated areas
- No cars left overnight unless staying in B&B
- $1,000 damage deposit required (check or credit card on file)
- Social media live streaming requires approval

### Venue Rental Only Notes

- Dessert plates, napkins, and silverware NOT provided by caterer unless arranged
- No staff on site for cutting cake - arrange your own
- Bring your own team for all setup
