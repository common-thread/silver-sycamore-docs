# Shoe Game Questions

A fun reception activity where the couple sits back-to-back, each holding one of their own shoes and one of their partner's shoes. The MC reads questions and the couple raises the shoe of whoever they think the answer applies to.

## Getting to Know Each Other

- Who wakes up first?
- Who stays up the latest?
- Who is more organized?
- Who exercises the most?
- Who takes up more than half of the bed?
- Who is the nerdiest one?
- Who is more outgoing?
- Who has the best taste in music?
- Who has the best taste in movies?
- Who cries during movies?
- Who is better at keeping secrets?
- Who has a better fashion sense?

## Relationship Questions

- Who made the first move?
- Who said "I love you" first?
- Who is a better kisser?
- Who plans the best date nights?
- Who spends the longest getting ready for a date?
- Who gives better gifts?
- Who is more romantic?
- Who loves who the most?
- Who wears the pants in the relationship?

## Daily Life

- Who does the household chores?
- Who is a better cook?
- Who is a better driver?
- Who talks the loudest?
- Who talks the most?
- Who spends all the money?

## Personality Traits

- Who is more stubborn?
- Who is the most stubborn?
- Who is funnier?
- Who is a better dancer?
- Who is a better singer?
- Who is more adventurous?
- Who is the smartest?
- Who is more competitive?

## Fun and Silly

- Who is most likely to binge-watch a show?
- Who would win in a hotdog eating contest?
- Who loves animals more?
- Who has the sweetest tooth?
- Who would survive longest in the jungle?
- Who believes in aliens?
- Who is more likely to get lost while driving?
- Who is more likely to break a bone?
- Whose shoe stinks more?
- Who looks the best today?
