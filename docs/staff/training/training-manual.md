# Training Manual

Comprehensive training guide for Silver Sycamore administrative assistants. This manual covers all core responsibilities from daily operations to event coordination.

## Position Overview

Administrative assistants are responsible for:

1. Maintain a clean office, sweeping floors each morning
2. Answer multiple phone lines and take messages
3. Set up tour appointments when requested by sales rep
4. Inventory control and order office supplies as needed
5. Order materials for upcoming events, confirm vendors, arrange staff
6. Book and coordinate showers
7. Coordinate events
8. Research products and necessities for venue
9. Provide technical support
10. Handle all B&B booking and check-in information
11. Confirm tour appointments and issue receipts
12. Receive payments for events and issue receipts

## Daily Cleaning Duties

The first and last 15 minutes of each work day are reserved for general cleaning and maintenance.

1. Sweep and vacuum floors
2. Dust desks, chairs, windowsills
3. Clean bathroom - no trash in bin, toilet, sink, and mirror should be spot free
4. Check that blinds are open and lights are on (no bulbs burnt out)
5. Start coffee machine

**Important:** This is the first room guests see - it must make a remarkable first impression.

## Answering Phones

You are the first point of contact for guests, whether in person or on the phone. Always greet with a smile and use polite, professional language.

### Phone Greeting

> "Good Morning/Afternoon, thank you for calling Silver Sycamore. My name is _________, how may I assist you?"

### Taking Messages

If you are unable to answer their question, collect the following information:

1. First and last name
2. A good callback number (and email if possible)
3. Detailed description of their question(s) or need(s)
4. Event date (if applicable)
5. Guest count (if applicable)
6. Reassure the guest that you will pass the message to the appropriate person and someone will be in touch as soon as possible

## Setting Up Tour Appointments

Check the answering machine each morning and ensure each message is recorded and delivered to the appropriate staff member.

### Information to Collect

1. First and last name (bride and groom)
2. Date they are wanting to book
3. Budget
4. Guest count
5. Indoor or outdoor ceremony preference
6. Email address
7. Phone number

**Tips:**
- Allow enough time between appointments - some may take longer than others
- Use the phone script to help determine what information you need and whether an appointment is needed

## Office Supplies

Maintain supplies for Silver Sycamore. Typically an Office Max order is made once or twice a month.

1. Make a list as supplies diminish
2. Prioritize items that are needed more quickly
3. Order through Office Depot

## Event Coordination

For each event you are assigned, you may need to:

1. Arrange for third-party vendors (preferred: One Stop Party Supply)
2. Order cakes for showers
3. Arrange for a DJ
4. Organize staff for the event
5. Research rental prices for extra items (wine glasses, tents, etc.)
6. Process invoices and payments ASAP

## Shower Bookings

### Booking Process

1. Print out a Shower Booking Form
2. Fill out as much information as possible (total pricing added after tax and gratuity)
3. Find package pricing in: Silver Sycamore > Packages > Showers
4. Take a deposit
5. Make a copy of the receipt and form of payment (if by check)

### Contract Process

**If client is present:**
1. Have them fill out the contract during booking

**If booking over the phone:**
1. Fill out as much information as possible on the contract
2. Send via email using Adobe Acrobat Fill & Sign
3. Location: Silver Sycamore > Events > Showers > Shower Contract

## Coordinating Events

### After Booking

1. Make a file for the shower
2. Get file to accounting to create an invoice

### 10-Day Out Appointment

1. Hold the 10-day out appointment to finalize details
2. Take the guaranteed guest count
3. Complete an event sheet for the kitchen
4. Follow "after shower" steps in binder

### Wedding Coordination

After booking a wedding:

1. Send month docs ("what to do")
2. Answer customer questions throughout planning
3. Set up 2-week out appointment
4. Follow "what to do after a wedding" procedures
5. See "day of coordination" in form of a wedding

## Research

Doing research on competition, comparing prices, and looking up local events is crucial.

1. Use all resources: internet, social media, etc.
2. Create a Word document with findings
3. Copy and paste information found
4. List sources for all research

## Technical Support

For technical support:

1. Check warranty/product manuals in filing cabinet (folder labeled "Important Product Manuals")
2. Reference appropriate folders and numbers for assistance

## Bed and Breakfast Reservations

We receive reservations through four channels. For each reservation, fill out the form in the Bed and Breakfast binder.

### By Phone

1. Have the booking form ready to collect all information
2. Add to Rezovations, Booking.com, and Google Calendar

### Through Bedandbreakfast.com

1. You will receive an email with most information
2. Reach out to customer for any additional information needed
3. Add to Booking.com and Google Calendar (B&B.com is linked with Rezovations - adds automatically)

### Through Rezovation

1. Email is received
2. Sign into Rezovations in Internet Explorer
3. Find the reservation and open to document credit card information

### Through Booking.com

1. Go to Occupancy > Reservation > Payments > Credit Card Holds > Card on File > Reveal
2. Add to Rezovations and Google Calendar

### Adding a Guest to Rezovations

1. Collect information for form
2. View credit card details - **notify guest**
3. You have 10 days to collect card info
4. Add to Booking.com and Google Calendar
5. In Rezovations, click on dates and add all information (guests, room rate)
6. In Booking.com, select room being booked, check sheets, rates and availability
7. In Google Calendar, add room name, guest name, check-in time, breakfast guest count, dietary needs

### After Receiving Reservation

1. Call customer to collect any missing information (CVV, billing zip, arrival time, guest count, dietary needs, room extras)
2. Send confirmation email through Rezovations: Occupancy > Reservation > Email > Confirmation Email

### Day Before Check-In

1. Send second email with entrance code attachment
2. Create file: B&B Folder > B&B Confirmation Temp > Fill in top section > Save as PDF
3. Write a thank you note expressing excitement to have them stay
4. Include parking details (especially for Sycamoreville rooms)
5. Include breakfast hours, location, and coupon info
6. For Salon rooms, include exterior lock instructions

### Day of Arrival

1. Make sure room is prepared properly (see B&B checklist)
2. Leave appropriate number of breakfast coupons (1 per morning)
3. Add referral program cards (print on card stock, double-sided with logo on back)

## Guest Check-In Process

### Receiving Payment

1. If reservation is through Bedandbreakfast.com, log in and physically check the guest in
2. If through Rezovations or Booking.com, a credit card must be present (unless paying cash)
3. For cash payment, a credit card still needs to be on file for incidentals

### After Check-Out

1. Send a review request via email (see: Silver Sycamore > Events > Review Request Script)
2. Add guest to Constant Contact: Log in > Add New > Enter name and email > Add to B&B Customers list
3. Add file to Jackie's folder on desk for payment confirmation
4. File goes to accounting for sales tax
5. Arrange housekeeping to clean rooms (text usually works best)

## Confirming Appointments and Reservations

1. Confirm appointments 1-2 days before the scheduled date
2. Use the phone number noted in the appointment detail box in the calendar
3. Apply same process to reservations

## Receiving Payments

1. Issue a receipt for all payments
2. Make copies of both the payment (if by check) and receipt
3. Write date of payment and date of event
4. Give guest their receipt
5. Copy goes to event coordinator/office manager
6. Payment goes to Jackie
