# Service Protocols

Service standards and operational procedures for Pine Street Cafe waitstaff. Follow these 23 steps for every guest to ensure a consistent, high-quality dining experience.

## Core Principle

**Be kind and smile.** Speak clearly and project your voice.

## 23 Points of Service

1. **Greet guest** - Provide a warm welcome as they enter
2. **Seat with menu and silverware** - Escort to table and provide menus
3. **Suggest a fun drink and tell of specials** - Promote beverage program and soup of the day
4. **Get drink order** - Place order on a ticket immediately
5. **Place alcohol order with bar** - Communicate bar orders promptly
6. **Take out drinks** - Serve beverages to table
7. **Take meal order** - Listen carefully and write clearly
8. **Repeat order** - **Critical step** - Confirm entire order with guest and ask if correct
9. **Make sure ticket prints in kitchen** - Verify ticket printed at kitchen station
10. **Take out condiments needed for order** - Anticipate and provide necessary accompaniments
11. **Refill drinks before order goes out** - Keep beverages topped off
12. **Check order for correctness at the window** - Quality check before delivery, write time out on order ticket
13. **Take out order** - Serve ladies first, position protein in front of guest
14. **Ask if anything else is needed** - Check for additional requests
15. **2 minute/2 bite taste test** - **Critical quality check** - Ask guest if all is to their liking after 2 minutes
16. **Refill drinks** - Maintain beverage service
17. **Tell guests of dessert for the day** - Promote dessert program
18. **Order dessert, clear plates, take out desserts** - Sequential dessert service
19. **Print ticket** - Tell guests you will take care of payment at the table when ready
20. **Clear plates if finished** - Remove finished dishware
21. **Check guests out at table** - Table-side payment processing
22. **Tell guests thank you** - Say "Thank you and come see us again soon"
23. **Clean table and floor** - Sanitize and reset for next guests

## Cash Drawer Policy

End of shift cash handling procedures.

1. Servers cannot leave until cash drawer has been counted and all cash and receipts are accounted for
2. Servers will be required to make up any shortage from their cash tips before leaving for the day
3. Each server must have a printed copy of each paid receipt for guest tickets that will be matched to the corresponding order from the kitchen printer

## Guest Check Payment Policy

Payment processing protocol for all servers.

### Before Taking Orders

1. Find out if guest check is to be split **before** taking orders

### Ticket Naming Convention

1. Use first initial of first and last name before table numbers on tickets
2. Format: **JS-2** (initials-table number)
3. For split checks, add letter suffix: **JS-4a, JS-4b, JS-4c, JS-4d**

### Before Printing the Bill

1. Look over the entire order to make sure you have charged for everything correctly
2. **You will be held responsible for all items not paid at the end of your shift**

### Presenting the Bill

1. Present the bill to the paying guest
2. Let them know you will take their payment at the table when ready

### Credit/Debit Card Payments

1. Complete all credit/debit card payments on your tablet
2. **Do not process at the front counter**
3. Server is responsible for swiping the card and ensuring payment processed
4. Verify with a printed receipt

### Cash Payments

1. Take cash payment to the register
2. Cash out the guest ticket
3. Count your change **twice**
4. Return change to guests
